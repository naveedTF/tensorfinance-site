About Tensor Finance Ltd

Tensor Finance Ltd provides mortgage and property finance support for individuals and property investors, delivering services through an authorised UK mortgage network.

The firm is led by a CeMAP-qualified professional with extensive experience in residential and investment finance. Tensor Finance is committed to delivering clear, reliable guidance tailored to clients’ personal and investment needs.

To provide calm, structured, and expert financial guidance that supports informed decision-making for homebuyers and property investors.

  • Support tailored to each client’s goals
  • Long-term client relationships built on trust
