Privacy Policy

Tensor Finance Ltd is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data in line with UK data protection legislation.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details including your name, contact information, financial details, and documentation required to assess mortgage or finance options.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide mortgage and finance support, assess product suitability, meet regulatory requirements, and communicate updates about your application.

Sharing Your Information

Your information may be shared with lenders, credit reference agencies, and our authorised mortgage network as required to process your enquiry. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, restrict, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.
