Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tensor Finance Ltd (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your data in compliance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Tensor Finance Ltd provides mortgage and property finance support to individuals and property investors. Regulated mortgage advice is delivered through an authorised UK mortgage network.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect personal data including:

  • Contact details (name, address, phone, email)
  • Financial information relevant to mortgage or finance applications
  • Identification documents (e.g., passport or driving licence)
  • Information required for regulatory checks and compliance

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide mortgage and property finance support
  • Prepare applications and submit them to lenders
  • Meet regulatory obligations under the FCA framework
  • Communicate with you about your enquiry or application

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your information under the following lawful bases:

  • Contract: to provide a service you request
  • Legal obligation: to meet FCA and anti-money-laundering requirements
  • Consent: where you explicitly agree
  • Legitimate interests: to operate and improve our services

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Our authorised mortgage network
  • Lenders or financial institutions where needed for applications
  • Compliance and regulatory bodies

We do not sell your data.

6. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of data (subject to regulatory requirements)
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

8. Retention

We keep your data only as long as required by law, generally up to six years after the end of a regulated relationship.

9. Contact

For privacy enquiries, email: info@tensorfinance.co.uk
