Privacy Policy

Tensor Finance Ltd is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data in line with UK data protection regulations.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal and financial information required to assess mortgage and finance options, including contact details, financial data, and relevant documentation.

How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely for providing mortgage and finance support, assessing product suitability, meeting regulatory obligations, and communicating updates related to your enquiry or application.

Sharing Your Information

We may share information with lenders, credit reference agencies, and our authorised mortgage network when necessary to process your application. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.
